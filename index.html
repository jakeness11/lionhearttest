Meet Your Coach

I started Lionheart Health & Fitness with a desire to guide people towards a lifestyle that will ultimately help them feel good mentally and physically. Together, we achieve this through custom-built exercise plans that are designed around your health, schedule, and goals.

What makes working with me different from most coaches is that you have my full support in implementing habits that are both manageable and sustainable. This allows you to create a fitter and healthier lifestyle, rather than feeling like you’re being used for a PT’s before & after portfolio, then forgotten about and left to return to former habits.
